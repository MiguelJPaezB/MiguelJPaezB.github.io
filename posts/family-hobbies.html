My Family and Hobbies

My family is very important to me. I currently live with my sister and my nephew. My brother lives with his wife and daughters, and my father lives at my grandmother’s house — we all live in Barranquilla.
My mother, however, lives in a small rural area called Las Tinas, in Corozal – Sucre. Even though we are far from each other, we always stay connected and support one another.

We enjoy spending time together, especially on weekends when we gather at my grandmother’s house to have lunch and talk. Sometimes we also go to the movies as a family.
They are my greatest motivation to keep learning and growing every day.

In my daily life, I do many things: I work at Kairós, I also design websites as a freelance web designer, I take care of my nephews, and I study.

My Favorite Hobbies

In my free time, I like doing activities that help me relax and learn new things.
One of my favorite hobbies is listening to music — it helps me concentrate and improve my English listening and pronunciation.

I also love drawing on my tablet. I usually learn digital art techniques through tutorials I find on YouTube or in online forums. I prefer to watch them in English because I can find what I need faster, and it also helps me improve my understanding of the language.

Another hobby I really enjoy is learning new songs. I play several instruments, but my favorite one is the guitar. I like searching for songs and taking the time to learn them — I try to play them by ear, although sometimes it’s a bit difficult.
Recently, the artists I listen to the most are Ed Sheeran, Imagine Dragons, and System of a Down.

My Presentation in English

This audio is a short personal introduction where I talk a little more about myself, my family, and what I like to do in my free time.

What I Learned

Talking about my family and hobbies helped me practice the use of present simple tense and possessive adjectives.

It also helped me improve my pronunciation while describing people and things I like.
This activity made me realize how English can connect my daily life with my learning goals.

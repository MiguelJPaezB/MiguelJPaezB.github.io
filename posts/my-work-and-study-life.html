My Work and Study Life

Managing my time between work and study has been one of the biggest challenges in my daily life. I currently work at Kairos and also as a freelance web designer. My days are full of projects, meetings, and tasks, but I always try to organize my time to keep learning and growing.

English plays a very important role in my professional life. Most of the tools, tutorials, and documentation I use are in English, so understanding the language helps me work faster and communicate better with clients and developers.

My Daily Routine

Every morning, I start my day early to check emails and organize the tasks I need to do. After that, I dedicate a few hours to work on website designs and coding. In the afternoon, I take time to study and complete my activities from the SENA program in Software Analysis and Development.

I try to keep a good balance between productivity and rest. Sometimes I take short breaks to listen to music or watch English videos that help me practice pronunciation and expand my vocabulary.

What I Learned

Writing about my work and studies helped me use the present simple tense and expressions related to routines and habits. It also helped me practice connecting ideas with transition words like after that, then, and finally.
